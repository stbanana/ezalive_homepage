EZ4000X is Ezalive's core product family, integrating bidirectional source/load capabilities for power electronics R&D and validation.

## Key Features

- AC/DC dual-mode output
- Bidirectional energy flow
- High-precision voltage/current control
- Adaptable to various test scenarios

## Product Functions

### Source Functions
- 0-300VAC adjustable voltage
- 10-200Hz adjustable frequency
- AC/DC/AC+DC/DC+AC modes
- 0~360° adjustable phase angle
- Harmonic and interharmonic simulation
- Custom waveform simulation

### Load Functions
- 10-500Hz frequency
- Custom current waveform simulation
- 1.414-5.0 adjustable crest factor
- Circuit topology simulation
- Arbitrary angle load/unload
- AC load current/power/power factor adjustment
- DC load CC/CR/CP/CV and 9 working modes

### Other Functions
- Parallel operation
- Single/three-phase switching
- 50th harmonic measurement
- USB/LAN/RS485 communication interfaces

## Typical Applications

- Inverter and converter testing
- Energy storage system validation
- E-drive system and motor control testing
