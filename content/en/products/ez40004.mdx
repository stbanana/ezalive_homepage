# EZ40004 Bidirectional AC/DC Source

EZ40004 is the flagship product from Ezalive, combining bidirectional source and load capabilities for power electronics R&D and validation.

## Key features

- AC/DC dual-mode output
- Bidirectional energy flow
- High-precision voltage/current control
- Flexible test scenarios

## Typical applications

- Inverter and converter testing
- Energy storage validation
- E-drive and motor control testing
